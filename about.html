About Us

Published by Casa Sunshine LLC

The Complete Journal is a perpetual life journal — not a diary that tracks days forward, but one that layers them, year over year, so the same days find each other across time. One sentence is all it takes to hold a moment forever.

What we make

The Complete Journal helps you capture your life one moment at a time: a sentence a day, the people and places that matter, your goals and milestones, and the memories you want to keep. It is designed to be simple, private, and lasting.

Who we are

The Complete Journal is developed and operated by Casa Sunshine LLC. We build tools that help people remember and reflect on the things that matter most.
